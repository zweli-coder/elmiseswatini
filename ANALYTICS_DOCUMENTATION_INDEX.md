## 📊 Feature Quick Reference

### The Three Charts

| Chart | Purpose | Data | Best For |
|-------|---------|------|----------|
| **Multi-Axis Line** | Track trends over months | Applications grouped by industry/month | Comparing seasonal patterns |
| **Scatter Plot** | Show day distribution | Applications by day-of-year | Finding recruitment peaks |
| **Industry Analysis** | Compare industry totals | Total applications per industry | Ranking industries |

### Key Features

| Feature | How to Use | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Year Selector** | Click dropdown, choose year | Filter data to specific year |
| **Hover Tooltips** | Hover over chart points | See detailed information |
| **Legend Toggle** | Click industry name in legend | Show/hide specific industries |
| **Download Button** | Click "Download" on any chart | Export chart as PNG image |
| **Statistics Display** | See at top of page | Quick overview of data volume |
